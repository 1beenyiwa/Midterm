Welcome to ConcordiaBook

ConcordiaBook is a cutting-edge web/mobile application designed for Concordia students to trade textbooks they no longer use for those they need. Connect with fellow students and make textbook exchanges easy!

How It Works

Register: Log in using your Concordia ID and password.

Add Textbooks You Have: Provide the title, year, edition, author, and course code for textbooks you no longer use.
